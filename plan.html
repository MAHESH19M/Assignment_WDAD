Goals

The goal of the Powell Fashion Clothing Shop website is to increase their buisness (sales and new customers) for the Leaves company by promoting their clothing line.

Success Evaluation

The number of customers and the revenue of the company will be recorded for a period of 3 months prior and after the site is launched,
so that any increase in sales and customers after the launch of the website should be attributable to the site. At the end of this 3 month period (and during),
the number of new customers and the increase in revenue will be counted and compared to the previous 3 months.

Target Audience

This site uses a minimalistic design and pleasing colors to attract Young families, mostly people around the age of 18 to 35,
and this is who the site is aimed at.
